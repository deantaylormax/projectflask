Model Background Description

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, 4 million Americans quit their jobs in July 2021(Ian Cook,September 2021,Harvard Business Review). Economists called it the “Great Resignation.”. High rate of employee turnover can lead the company to huge monetary losses. Therefore identifying and analyzing what factors are associated with employee turnover will allow companies to better understanding their employees and develop corrective measures. Our model can help provide some predictive insights to employers that what data factors may affect employees'decision.
